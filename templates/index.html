About Credit Card Fraud

Credit card fraud includes unauthorized transactions made using stolen card details or card-not-present data. Common patterns include unusually large amounts, rapid sequence purchases, or abnormal geographic origins. This console scores each transaction on fraud likelihood so investigators can prioritize alerts.
